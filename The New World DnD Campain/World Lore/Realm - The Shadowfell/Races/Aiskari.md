Beings of infinite dust and endless form, these beings defy normal forms. Endless swirling dust, these creatures talk entirely telepathically and do not share any facial or bodily features. 

Occasionally Aiskari who wish to interact with beings of other realms regularly try and "wear" humanoid clothes to help retain their form and help beings guide their focus  at something tangible

Being entirely telepathic beings, these creatures not only speak but hear telepathically, able to subconsciously hear and delve into the memories and thoughts of those around them - to an uncanny degree. Not even they can stop the thoughts, so even the most social Askari retreat to areas of low foot traffic and seclusion to not be overwhelmed with voices. 

As beings that defy typical form, shades of the Aiskari can easily split away from the original form to expand their telepathic range
